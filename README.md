### Hi there, I'm Michele 👋

I am a software engineer, full-stack developer, geek and Agile enthusiast. I love coding and problem solving. I started coding at a very young age, just for fun, creating programs in BASIC and Pascal. It’s been many years since I wrote my first line of code, and a few white hairs have started appearing in my beard. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to study and work with many systems, different programming languages and frameworks. Currently, I am involved in designing and developing software solutions, building applications for web, mobile and desktop.
